Case studies.

Real engineering challenges. Production-grade solutions. Measurable business impact.

A manufacturing company had no visibility into inventory, finance, and procurement. They were running operations on spreadsheets and facing costly errors and delays.

Solution — Custom ERP Platform

TaQnivo engineered a custom ERP system integrating inventory, purchase orders, accounts, and sales reporting into one real-time dashboard. Operational errors reduced by 70% and order processing time cut in half.

Solution — HRMS with AI Attendance

We delivered a full HRMS with automated payroll, biometric attendance integration, leave management, and statutory compliance. Integrated AI-powered anomaly detection reduced payroll errors to near-zero. HR processing time dropped by 80%.

A retail brand wanted to expand nationally with an online store and digital strategy. They had great products but zero digital presence or e-commerce capability.

Solution — E-Commerce + AI Marketing

We built a custom e-commerce platform, integrated AI-powered product recommendations, launched targeted advertising campaigns, and managed their social media. The brand crossed ₹1 crore in online revenue within its first year.
